Make a Difference with Your Donation

Every contribution you make helps us provide education, food, healthcare, and empowerment programs to underprivileged communities. Your donation can transform lives and bring hope to those in need.

At Navodaya, we ensure that your funds are utilized efficiently to create the maximum impact. With your support, we can continue our mission of building a better future for all.

Why Your Donation Matters

Millions of people in India still struggle with poverty, lack of education, and inadequate healthcare. Your donation is more than just money—it’s an investment in a brighter future.

With your help, we can:

  • Provide Quality Education: Many children drop out due to financial constraints. Your donation helps fund scholarships, school supplies, and digital learning tools.
  • Offer Healthcare Services: We conduct medical camps, provide life-saving medicines, and support hospitals in rural areas.
  • Empower Women: Training programs in stitching, IT skills, and self-help groups enable women to earn a sustainable income.
  • Feed the Hungry: Hunger is a pressing issue. Your support helps us distribute meals to families in need.
  • Support Disaster Relief: During floods, earthquakes, and other crises, we provide emergency relief, shelter, and food supplies.
